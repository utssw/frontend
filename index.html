ASEW 2025

The Australian Software Engineering Week aims at providing a forum to software engineering researchers, students and practitioners to share, network and learn from each other through the presentation of their research and innovation. This week will provide important insights into future directions and share practices which worked, and which did not work including lessons learned and benefit realisation.
