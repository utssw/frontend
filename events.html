🧊 Day 1 - Foundations & Frontiers

Software Engineering as a Discipline in the Age of Agentic AI

Day 1 focuses on establishing the foundational principles of software engineering while exploring how these principles must evolve in response to the emergence of agentic AI systems. We'll examine both the theoretical underpinnings and practical applications of software engineering in this new paradigm.
