UTS SW 2025

UTS Software Week is an event organised by several universities aiming to enable industry involvement in university education. We intend to showcase various projects and the latest research formulated by students in order to bridge the gap between university research and industry needs. This week will provide opportunities to network with major industries to elevate businesses and personal growth.
